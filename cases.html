Case Study 1: Protecting a Startup's Innovation

Practice Area: Intellectual Property Law

The Challenge: Elijah developed AfyaNet, a healthcare app connecting patients with clinics and pharmacies. He needed to protect the innovation and brand.

Our Approach:

  • Trademark registration.
  • Copyright guidance for software.
  • Licensing and partnership agreement drafting.
  • Long-term IP strategy.

The Outcome: AfyaNet launched successfully and attracted investors while Elijah retained ownership and control.

Case Study 2: Resolving a Property Boundary Dispute

Practice Area: Conveyancing & Real Estate

The Challenge: A boundary dispute between neighboring farmers in Meru threatened livelihoods and local harmony.

Our Approach:

  • Land registry review.
  • Surveyor collaboration.
  • Historical ownership documentation.
  • Court representation.

The Outcome: The court clarified lawful boundaries in favor of our client and helped restore peace.

Case Study 3: Business Cyber Risk Response

Practice Area: Corporate & Commercial Law (Business)

The Challenge: A Nairobi startup suffered a major cybersecurity breach exposing customer data.

Our Approach:

  • Regulatory notification guidance.
  • Coordination with cybersecurity experts.
  • Litigation risk management.
  • Data protection policy upgrades.

The Outcome: Regulatory issues were resolved, customer confidence recovered, and security controls improved.

Case Study 4: Standing Up for Consumer Rights

Practice Area: Employment & Labor Law

The Challenge: Lydia Njeri bought a faulty appliance and the seller refused to honor warranty obligations.

Our Approach:

  • Reviewed purchase and warranty documents.
  • Filed complaint with relevant authority.
  • Initiated legal proceedings.

The Outcome: The seller replaced the product, compensated Lydia, and issued an apology.

Case Study 5: Improving Workplace Safety Through Legal Compliance

Practice Area: Tax Law

  • Settlement negotiations with affected workers.
  • Development of safety compliance policies.

The Outcome: The company improved compliance and reduced future workplace risk.
