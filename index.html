Case Study 1: Protecting a Startup's Innovation

Practice Area: Intellectual Property Law

The Challenge: Elijah developed AfyaNet, a healthcare app connecting patients with nearby clinics and pharmacies. As adoption grew, he needed to protect the idea and brand from copycats.

Our Approach:

  • Registered trademarks for the platform brand.
  • Advised on copyright protection for the software.
  • Drafted licensing and partnership agreements.
  • Built a long-term IP protection strategy.

The Outcome: Elijah launched AfyaNet, attracted investors, and retained ownership and control of his intellectual property.

Case Study 2: Resolving a Property Boundary Dispute

Practice Area: Conveyancing & Real Estate

The Challenge: Two neighboring farmers in Meru, John Mwiti and Peter Njoroge, disputed a property boundary, affecting livelihoods and community relations.

Our Approach:

  • Reviewed land registry records.
  • Worked with professional surveyors.
  • Gathered historical ownership documentation.
  • Represented our client in court.

The Outcome: The court ruled in favor of our client, clarified the lawful boundaries, and helped restore peace.

Case Study 3: Business Cyber Risk Response

Practice Area: Corporate & Commercial Law (Business)

The Challenge: A Nairobi-based startup suffered a major breach exposing customer data and threatening trust.

Our Approach:

  • Advised on regulatory notification obligations.
  • Coordinated with cybersecurity experts.
  • Managed legal risks linked to potential claims.
  • Supported stronger data protection policy implementation.

The Outcome: The startup resolved regulatory concerns, regained customer confidence, and strengthened its cybersecurity posture.

Case Study 4: Standing Up for Consumer Rights

Practice Area: Employment & Labor Law

The Challenge: Lydia Njeri purchased a refrigerator that failed shortly after purchase, and the seller refused to honor warranty terms.

Our Approach:

  • Reviewed purchase and warranty agreements.
  • Filed a formal complaint with the consumer authority.
  • Initiated legal proceedings.

The Outcome: The company replaced the appliance, compensated Lydia, and issued a formal apology.

Case Study 5: Improving Workplace Safety Through Legal Compliance

Practice Area: Tax Law

The Challenge: A manufacturing factory in Eldoret faced regulatory scrutiny after an accident involving unsafe equipment injured workers.

Our Approach:

  • Negotiated fair settlements with affected workers.
